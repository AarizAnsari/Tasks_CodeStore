Types of Lists

There are three types of Lists used in HTML:

Unordered List

Unordered List displays it's items in a bullet points format and no serial order is provided to the items.

  • January
  • February
  • March
  • April
  • May
  • June

Ordered List

Ordered List displays it's items in a numeric format and a serial order is provided to the items.

  1. January
  2. February
  3. March
  4. April
  5. May
  6. June

Description List

Description List displays it's items along with a description provided by the developer.

January:
It is the first month of the year
February:
It is the second month of the year
March:
It is the third month of the year
April:
It is the fourth month of the year
May:
It is the fifth month of the year
June:
It is the sixth month of the year
